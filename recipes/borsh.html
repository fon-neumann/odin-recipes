Borscht

This borscht recipe is suitable for vegetarians, yogis, and those who watch their diet. The borscht has a bright and rich flavor and is perfect for a full and energetic life.

  • Water - 3 liters
  • Canned red beans - 1 can
  • Beet - 2 medium
  • Potatoes - 3 big
  • Carrot - 1
  • Cabbage - 1/4 of a big cabbage
  1. Start boiling water and a tablespoon of salt
  2. Cut the vegetables and add them in the following order: potatoes, cabbage, carrot, beet
  3. In the end add the canned red beans and some spices to taste. Turn off the heat and wait for 30 minutes. Borscht is ready.
  4. Serve with sourcream, parsley and dill
