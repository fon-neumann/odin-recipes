Pancakes

The recipe for these pancakes it is suitable for a vegetarian diet, when people don't eat eggs, but can take milk. For vegan diet, water can be used instead of milk.

  • Wheat flour - 1 cup
  • Milk - 2 cups
  • Sugar - 1 tbsp.
  • Soda - 1/2 tsp.
  • Salt - 1 tsp.
  • Vegetable oil - 50 ml
  1. Pour milk into a bowl.
  2. Add the flour, sugar, salt and baking soda. Stir everything with a whisk. Leave the dough for about thirty minutes.
  3. Lightly oil a hot frying pan and pour in a small amount of dough. Rotate the pan to distribute the batter evenly and bake. After frying on one side flip the pancake to the other side.
  4. Serve with any kind of filling: sourcream, butter, skimmed milk, chockolate, etc.
